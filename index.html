The Hollow Shouts

The Hollow Shouts are an up-and-coming Irish alternative rock band originating from Dublin, Ireland. Their debut album is set to be released in December of 2025, following their single and title track “Echoes on the Liffey.” Their sound draws heavily from the gothic post-punk movement in Europe, with gritty guitars, rumbling basslines, and a brooding sonic atmosphere. The band cites modern Irish post-punk groups such as Fontaines D.C. and The Murder Capital as major touchstones, admiring their raw portrayals of contemporary Ireland and their intense, oetic vocal deliveries. They also draw inspiration from classic pioneers like Joy Division, whose minimalist darkness and rhythmic urgency helped shape The Hollow Shouts’ approach to mood, structure, and emotional restraint.

While their lyrics are rooted in their personal experiences growing up in Ireland—exploring alienation, faith, generational tension, and the shifting identity of modern Dublin—the band channels these themes through a distinctly post-punk lens. With momentum building around their debut album, The Hollow Shouts have already announced plans for an early-2026 European tour, including shows across Ireland, the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands, with a North American leg expected to follow later in the year as their international profile continues to rise.
