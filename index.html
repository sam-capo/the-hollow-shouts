The Hollow Shouts

The Hollow Shouts are an up-and-coming Irish alternative rock band originating from Dublin, Ireland. Their debut album is set to be released in December of 2025 following their single title track “Echoes on the Liffey”. They note key influences from the gothic post-punk movement in Europe, echoing the sounds of gritty guitars and heavy basslines. While their lyrics are rooted in their experiences growing up in Ireland, exploring topics of alienation, faith, and modern Dublin.
